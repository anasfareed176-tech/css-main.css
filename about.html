About MojoBoost

MojoBoost helps Android Minecraft players get the most FPS out of their device by recommending the right performance mods for their exact setup.

🎯 Why MojoBoost?

Playing Minecraft Java Edition on Android through Mojo Launcher or PojavLauncher is amazing — but the performance can vary wildly depending on which mods you install. Installing the wrong mod, or missing a critical one, can mean the difference between 10 FPS and 60 FPS.

MojoBoost takes the guesswork out. Enter your device specs, select your goals, and get a personalized list of mods that are guaranteed to be compatible and optimized for your hardware.
