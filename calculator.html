💡 FPS Tips for Mobile

📐
Lower Render Distance

Dropping from 12 to 6 chunks can double your FPS on low-end devices.

🎨
Use Fast Graphics

Turn off fancy graphics, smooth lighting, and clouds for big FPS boosts.

🧠
Allocate More RAM

In Mojo Launcher, allocate 1.5–2 GB RAM for best performance without crashes.

🌡️
Keep Device Cool

Thermal throttling can cut FPS in half. Take breaks or use a cooling pad.
